# NerveOS — Login Wallpaper Prompts 🎬

Use these prompts to generate 7 dynamic backgrounds for the Secure Access screen. 
**Core Instruction:** Each image MUST have the name of the day rendered within the scene in a futuristic, digital, or industrial font (like a HUD or stamped onto hardware).

**Recommended Style:** Cinematic, Industrial, Cyberpunk, 8K, Photorealistic, High-Contrast.

---

### MONDAY — "The Factory Floor"
> **Prompt:** A top-down view of a futuristic automated factory assembly line, robotic arms moving in high-exposure blur. Render the text **"MONDAY"** as a glowing holographic projection over the circuitry. Cold blue and slate grey lighting, hyper-realistic, technical aesthetic.

### TUESDAY — "Logic Grid"
> **Prompt:** A vast digital grid of neon green laser lines. Render the text **"TUESDAY"** in a green monospaced digital font, glitching slightly in the center of the horizon. Floating geometric wireframe structures, data-stream particles, matrix-style deep shadows.

### WEDNESDAY — "Deep Neural Link"
> **Prompt:** A macro shot of a glowing bio-mechanical brain core with intricate neural pathways. Render the text **"WEDNESDAY"** etched into a metal plate near the core. Amber and gold fiber optic cables, dark liquid cooling background, cinematic depth of field.

### THURSDAY — "Hardware Architecture"
> **Prompt:** A cross-section of a high-end futuristic motherboard. Render the text **"THURSDAY"** in a glowing teal "stencil" font directly onto one of the large capacitors. Cinematic teal and orange lighting, macro photography, industrial hardware feel.

### FRIDAY — "High Energy Glitch"
> **Prompt:** A cyberpunk city street during a digital rain storm. Render the text **"FRIDAY"** as a massive, flickering neon billboard in the background. Intense magenta and electric blue contrast, motion blur, raw energy, cinematic action shot.

### SATURDAY — "Aesthetic Orbit"
> **Prompt:** A cinematic view of Earth's horizon from a low-orbit terminal. Render the text **"SATURDAY"** in a clean, white minimalist HUD font floating on the satellite screen. Glowing city lights at night, soft starlight, serene but high-tech.

### SUNDAY — "The Maintenance Void"
> **Prompt:** A minimalist dark void with a single glowing terminal pillar. Render the text **"SUNDAY"** as a simple white digital readout on the pillar's screen. Brutalist architecture, extreme shadow contrast, white spotlight, peaceful yet powerful.
